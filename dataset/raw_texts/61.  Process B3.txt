The MPON reports the meter operation to the GO. The GO examines the application of the MPON. The GO rejects the application of the MPON or the GO confirmes the application of the MPON. The GO informs about the registration confirmation of the MPON for the MPOO. The GO informs about the registration confirmation of the MPON for the MSPO. The MPON and the MPOO perform the equipment acquisition and/or equipment changes. The MPON informs  about the failure of the entire process for the GO or the MPON informs  about the successful completion of the entire process for the GO. The GO informs about the failure of the overall transaction by deadline for the MPON if after a maximum time limit no message of the MPON is present at the GO. If the MPON informs about the failure of the entire process for the GO, the GO confirms the failure of the assignment to the MPON. If the MPON informs about the successful completion of the overall process for the GO, the GO assigns the MPON. The GO confirms the assignment to the MPON. The GO informs about the failure of the assignment of the MPON for the MPOO or the GO informs  about the assignment of the MPON for the MPOO. The GO informs  about the failure of the assignment of the MPON for the MSPO or the GO informs about the assignment of the MPON for the MSPO. The GO informs about the assignment of the MPON for the SP.
