You can enter a topic that interests you, and how much money you want. You can also enter experience and hobbies. Several offers will arrive to you (at arbitrary points in time). You can accept or deny offers. As soon as an offer is accepted to you,  all other offers become invalid. You have to write at least 3 status updates about your internship (every week). The company has to write 3 status updates about you. After the internship is finished you can recommend the company to a list of friends (via twitter). Separate tweets are sent in parallel.
